### Tip Pin 1: 5 Minute Bedtime Routine
**Title:** The 5 Minute Bedtime Story Routine That Works
**Content:**
1. Dim the lights
2. Get cozy (blanket, lovey)
3. Read slowly, voice low
4. Pause at pictures
5. End with same phrase every night

**Hashtags:** #bedtimeroutine #sleeptips #toddlersleep #parentinghacks #bedtimestories

- They feel smart knowing what's next
- It becomes a comfort ritual
- Eventually they'll move on!

## PIN DESIGN SPECS

**Optimal Size:** 1000 x 1500 px (2:3 ratio)
**File Type:** PNG or JPG
**Text:** Large, readable, max 6 words in main headline

**Brand Colors:**
- Primary: Soft purple (#9B8AA5)
- Secondary: Warm cream (#FFF8E7)
- Accent: Gentle gold (#D4AF37)
- Text: Dark charcoal (#333333)

**Fonts:**
- Headlines: Rounded, friendly (like Nunito, Quicksand)
- Body: Clean sans-serif (like Open Sans)
